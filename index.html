about us

Inova is a company that has a strong focus on sparse innovation. Innovations that are cheap and easy. We are a newly formed company that works to create innovation that is cheap and easy.

We will send out a message to all new young innovators to think about sparse innovation in the future. To achieve this, we will have an innovation camp regularly on how to think frugally when coming up with the new idea. We want to show the world our experiences with frugal innovation.

In addition, we accept your ideas and support the innovation leader.
